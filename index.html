    Students Expectations

    Survey Question 1. "What were your expectations before your first use of our Chinese learning chatbots?" The question above addressed student feedback regarding expectations. It asked for qualitative responses.

    Among the three classes, 10 of 27 (37%) students reported positive expectations (e.g., finding the chatbot useful, intuitive, or exciting) before using the Chinese learning chatbots;. 10 of 27 (37%) students reported negative expectations (e.g., confusing, difficult in conversing) before using the Chinese learning chatbots.

    Class 2101 reported the greatest rate (50%) of positive expectation. Class 1101 reported the second greatest rate (44%) of positive expectation. Class 1102 reported the least great rate (0%) of positive expectation; instead, 50% of students reported neutral in class 1102.

    Class 1102 reported the greatest rate (50%) of negative expectation. Class 2101 reported the second greatest rate (42%) of negative expectation. Class 1101 reported the least great rate (22%) of negative expectation.

    Skills Benifits

    Survey Question 11. "Please rank the 5 language skills in terms of benefits from practicing with our chatbots this semester and why (5: most helpful; 1: least helpful)?"

    Among the three classes, the average benefits score for speaking is 4.3. The average benefits score for listening is 3. The average benefits score for reading is 3.2. The average benefits score for pronunciation is 4.3. The average benefits score for character recognition is 2.9.
